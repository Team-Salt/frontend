SeqGAN: Sequence generative adversarial nets with policy gradien

L Yu, W Zhang, J Wang, Y Yu 31st AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI 2017) one line brief introduction here.
Cited by: 1222 Year: 2017 View: 1011

Weinan Zhang


Shanghai Jiaotong University

Generative adversarial nets (GANs) have been widely studied during the recent development of deep learning and unsupervised learning. With an adversarial training mechanism, GAN manages to train a generative model to fit the underlying unknown real data distribution under the guidance of the discriminative model estimating whether a data instance is real or generated. Such a framework is originally proposed for fitting continuous data distribution such as images, thus it is not straightforward to be directly applied to information retrieval scenarios where the data is mostly discrete, such as IDs, text and graphs...
